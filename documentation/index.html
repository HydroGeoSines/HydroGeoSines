HydroGeoSines

Dynamic Earth: Hydrologic responses to natural and human forcings

A collaborative effort to develop, discuss, and share methods about how and why surface and groundwater respond to internal and external perturbations, including

  • Precipitation
  • Barometric pressure
  • Surface loading
  • Seismic events
  • Earth and ocean tides
  • Soil moisture and evapotranspiration
  • Aquifer pumping and recharge

Project Elements

  1. Data Exploration and Selection
    • Synthetic datasets
    • Field datasets
    • User-defined datasets
  2. Data Preprocessing
    • Parsing
    • Gaps, steps, spikes, outliers
    • Absolute vs gauge pressure
    • Open vs shut-in boreholes
    • Ambient vs freshwater head
  3. Time Domain Analysis
    • Auto- and cross-correlations
    • Regression deconvolution
  4. Frequency Domain Analysis
    • Spectral analysis
    • Wavelet analysis
    • Spectral density and coherence
  5. Postprocessing
    • System identification
    • Response function interpretation
